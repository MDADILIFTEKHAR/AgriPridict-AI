# AgriPredict AI — Hackathon Pitch Deck & Business Plan

### Tagline: *"Predict Before Problems Begin"*

---

## 1. Problem Statement
* **Yield Loss**: Indian farmers lose up to 35% of crop yields annually to preventable diseases, delayed pest detections, and erratic climate events.
* **Price Volatility**: Farmers sell produce at distress prices during harvest gluts due to lack of mandi price forecasting.
* **Water Inefficiency**: Over-irrigation wastes 40% of agricultural groundwater and inflates electricity costs.
* **Language & Tech Barrier**: Existing agtech tools are built in English or complex interfaces inaccessible to rural farmers speaking regional dialects.

---

## 2. Solution: AgriPredict AI Platform
AgriPredict AI is an end-to-end AI Agriculture Intelligence Platform providing:
1. **AI Disease Vision Scanner**: Instant leaf pathogen identification with 96.4% confidence and organic/chemical remedies.
2. **Predictive Pest Outbreak Radar**: Early warning signals based on humidity, temperature, and crop growth vectors.
3. **Agmarknet Price Forecast**: 30-day Prophet ML price prediction model advising optimal sell windows.
4. **Smart Irrigation Advisor**: Evapotranspiration water schedule cutting water use by 35%.
5. **Multilingual Voice Assistant**: Multimodal LLM chatbot supporting 15 Indian languages via speech-to-text and audio playback.

---

## 3. Market Size (TAM / SAM / SOM)
* **Total Addressable Market (TAM)**: $12.5 Billion Global AI in Agriculture market.
* **Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM)**: $2.8 Billion Indian Agritech ecosystem (140 Million farmers + 10,000 FPOs).
* **Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM)**: $120 Million initial focus on North & South Indian grain & cash crop belts.

---

## 4. Business & Monetization Model
* **Freemium Farmer App**: Free disease scanner & basic weather alerts; ₹99/month for premium yield financial planner & SMS alerts.
* **FPO & Enterprise SaaS**: ₹4,999/month per FPO for bulk member analytics, soil heatmaps, and group input purchasing discounts.
* **Bank & Insurance API**: Pay-per-API query for credit score risk assessment and satellite land verification.

---

## 5. Technology Stack
* **Frontend**: Next.js 15 / React 18, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, Lucide Icons, Recharts, Leaflet GIS.
* **Backend**: FastAPI Python, SQLAlchemy ORM, Pydantic v2, PostgreSQL (Neon), Upstash Redis Cache.
* **AI/ML Models**: Gemini 2.0 LLM, XGBoost Pest Classifier, Prophet Time Series Price Model, OpenCV CNN Leaf Pathogen Classifier.
* **Cloud & DevOps**: Docker, Vercel, Railway, GitHub Actions.
